Scheduling & attending sessions
What you need to know before you go

Session scheduling is open now. Log in to the attendee dashboard ↗ with your Adobe ID to build your schedule.

Do I need to schedule sessions in advance? ▼
Yes. To secure a seat in any session, you must add it to your personal Summit schedule. Sessions and labs may fill up, so schedule them as soon as possible.
Will my seat be held if I pre-enrolled? ▼
Your seat will be reserved until three minutes before the session start time. If you fail to arrive three minutes prior, your seat will be forfeited to someone in the onsite wait line. Please arrive early — your enrollment will be validated and scanned at entry.
What are the waitlist policies? ▼
Online waitlist: If a session is sold out, add yourself to the online waitlist. If a seat opens up, you'll be automatically added and notified via email.

Onsite wait line: If you're not pre-enrolled, stand in the onsite wait line outside the session room. Arrive 15–20 minutes prior. Two minutes before the session starts, the door monitor will allow wait-line attendees in as space allows.

Note: waitlists will be cleared three weeks prior to Summit — you'll be notified and advised to sign up for other available sessions.
Can room assignments change? ▼
Yes. Room assignments will be displayed in the session catalog and on your schedule the week before the event. Access your schedule via the Summit mobile app before arriving to identify session rooms.
What do I need to bring to sessions and labs? ▼
Sessions: Bring something to take notes with — pen and paper, tablet, or laptop.

Labs: All required equipment and files will be provided. In limited cases you may be asked to bring your own laptop — this will be communicated in advance. Read the lab description thoroughly for any specific requirements.
Will session materials be available after Summit? ▼
Adobe will post presentations and materials for sessions where permitted. Materials will be available for download via the session catalog when logged in. Some speakers are bound by copyright or licensing agreements and cannot make materials available. In-person lab materials are available to in-person attendees only.
How do I delete or swap a session? ▼
Log in to the attendee dashboard and navigate to My Schedule. From there you can remove or swap sessions directly from the schedule or catalog view.
